About Us

Founded in 2024 by four siblings, E Suenug is a proud continuation of our mother's recipe from 2017. Our name honors the Suluk word "sunug" (bakar) combined with our mother's name "Sue" — a tribute to our culture and family legacy.

Our Menu

We serve freshly grilled Satay Ayam, Daging, Kambing, Sayap Bakar, Pantolan, Buntut, Kulit, Pedal and more! Most items at only RM1, Sayap Bakar at RM2.50 — freshly prepared every day.

Contact & Delivery

We open daily from 3:30 PM to 8:00 PM. Personal delivery available — charges based on distance. Contact us early to place your orders!
